Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

MAIA Creative House ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at maiacreativehouse.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Analytics data — if you consent, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymised information about how you use our site (pages visited, time on site, general location by country, device type). Your IP address is anonymised and we never identify you personally.
  • Contact form submissions — if you reach out via our contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message through Formspree, a third-party service. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry.

We do not collect any information if you decline cookies.

2. Cookies

We use cookies only if you give your consent. The only cookies we set are Google Analytics cookies, which help us understand site traffic in aggregate. No personal data is stored in these cookies.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or cookies. You can also manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To understand how visitors find and use our website, so we can improve it
  • To respond to enquiries sent through our contact form
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Google Analytics (Google LLC) — website analytics. Data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — contact form processing. Data is handled in accordance with Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — website hosting. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers; see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion — you can request that we delete your personal data
  • Objection — you can object to how we process your data
  • Portability — you can request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — you can withdraw cookie consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@maiacreativehouse.com.

6. Data Retention

Contact form enquiries are kept for as long as necessary to respond and for reasonable follow-up. Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics in accordance with our configured retention settings (14 months by default).

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
