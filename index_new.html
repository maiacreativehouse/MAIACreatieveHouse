The founder

I'm Güneş, a Brand Strategist & AI Marketing Consultant who has spent 10+ years building brands across tech, wellness, and consumer. Brands that feel inevitable. I use AI as a creative and strategic multiplier, helping brands and their teams work faster, think sharper, and build marketing systems that scale without losing the human touch. I've learned that the most powerful brands don't explain themselves. They make you feel something before you read a word.

MAIA is named for one of the Pleiades stars, a quiet tribute to my late mother Pervin, whose name in Turkish means Pleiades. It's a reminder that what we build should carry meaning beyond what's visible.

I work with a select number of clients each quarter, founders and brands across San Francisco, New York, and globally, who are ready to build something that lasts.
